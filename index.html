Origin of Christmas

The Christmas story is related to the birth of Jesus, although it is not clear what day He was born. This is a Christian celebration, but over time it has spread outside the religious sphere.

The celebration on December 25 was created by the church when it realized that pagan celebrations competed with religious celebrations.
